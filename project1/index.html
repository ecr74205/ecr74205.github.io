EXPLORE CENTRAL PA

Experience the Heart of Pennsylvania

Welcome to Central Pennsylvania – a region rich in natural beauty, small-town charm, and vibrant culture. Central PA offers something for every traveler. From outdoor adventures and family-friendly amusements parks, historical museums, and shopping, plan your perfect trip and experience the heart of Pennsylvania like never before.
