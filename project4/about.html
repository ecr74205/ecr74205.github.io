WHO WE ARE

Welcome to the Aurora Art Museum. Located in the heart of downtown, the museum’s light-filled galleries, sculpture garden, and café offer visitors a welcoming environment to slow down, reflect, and engage.

Founded in 1998, the museum has grown into one of the region’s most dynamic art spaces, Museum curates exhibitions that highlight both established and emerging artists. With rotating exhibits, interactive installations, and a permanent collection featuring over 2,000 pieces, every visit offers something new to explore.

OUR MISSION

We believe art has the power to illuminate, challenge, and inspire. Our mission is to provide a space where diverse voices are represented, ideas are exchanged, and every visitor feels a sense of belonging.

OUR COMMUNITY

The museum is more than a gallery—it’s a gathering place. Through educational programs, artist talks, family workshops, and community partnerships, we strive to make art accessible to all. Whether you’re an art student, a curious traveler, or a family looking for inspiration, the Aurora Art Museum has something for you.
